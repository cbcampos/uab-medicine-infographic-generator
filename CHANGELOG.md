# Changelog

## 2026.05.12

- Added deterministic audience-specific section plans for academic, clinical, patient, and community infographics.
- Added audience-specific required panel titles and prompt constraints.
- Added the Streamlit "All 4 audiences" generation mode with a downloadable contact sheet and individual image downloads.
- Improved qualitative/non-numeric source handling to reduce invented numbers, device readings, charts, and unsupported sample descriptors.
- Tightened patient/community framing to reduce overpromising, individual-blame language, and unsupported local-resource claims.
- Added text-density and scannability rules so generated infographics favor fewer panels, shorter bullets, larger type, and faster comprehension.
- Updated the refinements scan to evaluate the expected audience-specific panel title.
